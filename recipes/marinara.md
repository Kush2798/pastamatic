# Spaghetti Marinara with shrimp and other fun things
Author: Kate Hudson <@k88hudson>

## Ingredients

- Spaghetti
- Tomato sauce
- Shrimps
 -Garlic
 -Oil 

# Method

1. Head up tomato sauce
2. Cook shrimp with garlic and oil
3. Add to tomato sauce
4. Boil water
5. Add spaghetti
6. Cook for 6 minutes
7. Mix it all together
8. Yum!
